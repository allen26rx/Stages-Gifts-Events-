The Founder

Stages Gifts & Events

Stages Gifts & Events was born from a simple belief — that every moment deserves to be beautifully remembered. What began as a passion for crafting meaningful experiences has grown into Abuja's most distinctive event styling and instant photography brand.

From intimate proposals to large-scale corporate productions, we bring cinematic attention to every detail, ensuring that when the lights go down and the guests go home, the memories remain — framed, printed, and permanent.

Our Polaroid & Instant Film Photography Station is at the heart of what we do: giving guests a tangible piece of the moment to carry with them always.
